<h2>会場の様子</h2>
<p>▼会場内は平日にも関わらず大盛況といった感じ<br />
<a href="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_05.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_05.jpg" alt="" title="tukemenhaku_121010_05" width="448" height="336" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3322" /></a><br />
▼ラストオーダーの時間が近づくにつれ徐々に人は減っていました。<br />
<a href="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_06.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_06.jpg" alt="" title="tukemenhaku_121010_06" width="448" height="336" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3321" /></a><br />
▼第二陣の最終日のラストオーダー間近になると品切れとなるお店もいくつかありました。<br />
<a href="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_01.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_01.jpg" alt="" title="tukemenhaku_121010_01" width="448" height="336" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3326" /></a><br />

<a href="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_07.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_07.jpg" alt="" title="tukemenhaku_121010_07" width="448" height="598" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3320" /></a><br />
▼会場内ではビールを売っている場所や<br />
<a href="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_02.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_02.jpg" alt="" title="tukemenhaku_121010_02" width="448" height="336" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3325" /></a><br />
▼アイドルの巣もありました！<br />
<a href="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_03.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_03.jpg" alt="" title="tukemenhaku_121010_03" width="448" height="336" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3324" /></a><br />

▼なんと嬉しいことに<strong>大つけ麺博</strong>には写メブースまで用意されていました！<br />
<a href="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_04.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_04.jpg" alt="" title="tukemenhaku_121010_04" width="448" height="336" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3323" /></a><br />
▼写メブースで取った写真がこちら<br />
<a href="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_08.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_08.jpg" alt="" title="tukemenhaku_121010_08" width="448" height="336" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3318" /></a><br />
2つのライトに照らされてキレイに撮れました！<br />

<a href="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_09.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="http://kotalab.com/wp-content/uploads/tukemenhaku_121010_09.jpg" alt="" title="tukemenhaku_121010_09" width="448" height="336" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3319" /></a><br />
普通の烏龍茶と黒烏龍茶の2種類が売られてました。<br />
体の脂肪が気になる僕は体に脂肪がつきにくい黒烏龍茶を飲みます。</p>
