<h2>エツミ クッションボックスフレキシブルS</h2>
<p><img src="/wp-content/uploads/innerbag_130627_02-448x297.jpg" alt="innerbag_130627_02" width="448" height="297" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-7666" /><br />
